Product

Lulo aims to revolutionize digital product development by enabling the latest advancements in AI to highly skilled and demanding product managers and their teams. Our value proposition centers on intelligent data extraction from diverse sources, synthesis and guidance for decision-making, comprehensive monitoring of product evolution, and documentation automation.

Features


  • Intelligent extraction

    Collect and analyze key information from multiple sources (documents, team meetings, conversations with customers) and formats (text, images, audio, video) to provide valuable insights with less effort.

  • Synthesis and guidance

    Support the definition or identification of features, user stories, bugs, improvement opportunities, and technical debt, simplifying decision-making.

  • Monitoring and evolution

    Provide a 360° view of the current state of the product, along with generating timely recommendations for task prioritization and building effective teams.

  • Automatic documentation

    Dynamically generate and keep up to date essential documentation such as roadmaps, release notes, user guides, etc., keeping the entire team synchronized and focused on the most important.

  • Third-party integration

    Easily integrate with leading tools like JIRA, GitHub, Slack, Drive, and others, facilitating an unified and efficient workflow.
